About E-LAN

Biggest College Fest of the Year

E-LAN is the annual cultural and technical fest of our college, bringing together students from various institutions for three days of competitions, workshops, performances, and fun.

With over 50 events spanning across technical, cultural, and sports categories, E-LAN provides a platform for students to showcase their talents and create lasting memories.

This year, we're taking it to the next level with international artists, tech innovators, and exciting prizes worth over ₹5 lakhs!
