About E-LAN

MIT College's Biggest Annual Fest

E-LAN is the premier annual cultural and technical fest of MIT College, bringing together students from various institutions for three days of intense competitions, innovative workshops, spectacular performances, and unforgettable memories.

With over 50 events spanning across technical, cultural, literary, and sports categories, E-LAN provides a platform for students to showcase their talents, learn new skills, and create lasting connections.
